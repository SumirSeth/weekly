By Sumir Seth.

This is the start of my new project, The Weeklies.
With this project, I aim to improve productivity and add an element of reflection in my life. I also made this project completely by myself.
I have really high hope for this, but only time will tell how successful this was. For now, I want to show you the basic things I wish to cover with this.
Namely:

  • What all I did previous week
  • Whether I was able to do the things I planned on doing last week
  • What all I wish to do next week

This is how most of my weeklies are going to be structured.

For the next week.

    • Prepare for Periodic tests. Mainly - Language subjects, biology and history.
    • Stay consistent.
    • Start a book/Read more books.
    • Complete the ordered books(academics).

See you next week!
