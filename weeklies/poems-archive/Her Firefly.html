Poems Her Firefly

By Jahnavi Singh.

Running through the backyards, Dancing across the bushes, Flying through the oceans, Swirling around her body, The firefly says, The oceans call her nearby through the mellifluous waves of hope, The mountains are enthralled to see the highs and lows , Trees standing tall gently glance at her standing firm in the storms blows Chirpy birds soaring high through the winds call her along, Every morning the sun looks down at her for the passion, zest and tenacity, The scorching afternoon heat is shaken by the cool temperament she has, Moonlight falls with the blossoms is amazeballs for the life and coldness within, She calls me hers. And she is my firefly!
