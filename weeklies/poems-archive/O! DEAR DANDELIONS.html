Poems O! DEAR DANDELIONS

By Jahnavi Singh.

O! dear dandelions you're back again raining in my garden of fresh breeze. Flooded mind, rainbows of you and me, My eyes aren't red I don't want to tell you this. I know, you will be gone tomorrow, Hopefully, you bring me the sunshine every year, Bouncing of the sunshine in the dandelion field through the eyes most beloved, still amazes me. O! Dear dandelions, now you're here... So tell me it was for real, The peace, warmth, amazement, skipping of my beat...the lovely eyes were for real, I know you will be gone tomorrow, Fly with the wind and come back soon, Bring me next time with you, That's all I can ask you for, For you took it the last time and never returned. O! Dear dandelions bring me the sunshine soon...
