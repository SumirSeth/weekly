Poems CRUMBLED POSTCARD

By Jahnavi Singh.

Write to me on a crumbled postcard When waves hit you, And you have nowhere to go. They say to stay upright, But when breakers are high you can fall on the dark street, And I'll light up the corner for you. When the cacophony of their honks Makes it unendurable, Climb up the hill with all your colours to my ivories. When the apocalypse is near The shelter of my sunflower will always be dear. Here, comes the busy moving road Nagging you down, You know the place to take your crown. The silence of the firefly is killing the beauty Of the this heart which never fails to smile So write to me on a crumbled postcard Which has kept us alive.
