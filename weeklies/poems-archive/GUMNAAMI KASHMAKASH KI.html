Poems GUMNAAMI KASHMAKASH KI

By Jahnavi Singh.

Anjaan thi mulaqaat ye, Benaam si thi chaap ye, Zindagi ki kashmakash se gumnaam thi ye , jaan ye, Titli bann aayi, Titli bann gayi, Anjaan ho kar , Bekhabhar bann kar, ki chaap iske dhuljaayenge ek din , Hairaani mein chod mujhe jaati har roz wo, Har ek din aati ,chod jaati chaap wo, Har ek din dhuljaate chaap uske , Phir bhi har ek din aati sath chap wo, Issi kashmakash mein , Issi gumnaami mein, Hairaan kar gayi mujhe wo, Har roz dhoyein hain hazaaron chap maine Par benakaam hoon aaj main, Keh sakta hoon aaj main, Zindagi ki kashmakash se gumnaam thi ye, jaan nahi, Janti thi gumnaam hai ye duniya aur gumnaam hai wo ...., Iss gumnaami ne hai diya ab naam isse, Zindagi ki kashmakash se gumnaam thi ye jaan ye?
